📚 E-LIBRARY - NILACHAL POLYTECHNIC COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING, BHUBANESWAR

📖 Welcome to Our E-Library

The E-Library of Nilachal Polytechnic College of Engineering, Bhubaneswar, serves as a digital knowledge hub for students, faculty, and researchers. It provides access to a vast collection of academic resources, e-books, research papers, journals, and multimedia content, promoting self-learning and advanced research opportunities.

🔍 Navigation

✨ Features of the E-Library

  • Digital Resources Collection – Thousands of e-books, journals, and research papers.
  • Remote Access & User Authentication – Secure login system for faculty and students.
  • Advanced Search & Categorization – Search by subject, author, or publication year.
  • Multimedia Learning Resources – Video lectures, tutorials, and virtual labs.
  • Subscription to Online Journals & Databases – Access to IEEE, Springer, Elsevier, etc.
  • E-Books and Digital Libraries Integration – Collaboration with NDL and open-access resources.
  • Computerized Library Management System – Automated book issuing and cataloging.
  • Research & Plagiarism Detection Tools – Integration of Turnitin and Grammarly.
  • Student & Faculty Support Services – Online help desk and digital literacy programs.

📌 Conclusion

The E-Library of Nilachal Polytechnic College of Engineering, Bhubaneswar, is a step towards modern digital education. By integrating technology with education, it empowers students and faculty with unlimited knowledge access, enhancing research capabilities and academic excellence.
